WHY US FOODS


Restaurant success goes far beyond great food. At US Foods, we think deeper about your business – what’s working and what potential lies ahead.  
From finances to operations to driving traffic, we’re with you as partners on the whole journey. Helping you make it every step of the way.

Easy Ordering

Scoop

Scoop offers innovative, labor-saving products to inspire your culinary vision. Discover insightful ingredients, recipes, applications and more.

Food Fanatics

Food Fanatics is a movement of culinary experts and food people who share their love of food to inspire business success. Learn about the magazine and events here.

CHEF'STORE

US Foods® CHEF’STORE® is your destination for the best in restaurant equipment and supplies and serves both professional and amateur chefs alike. Come visit us!
